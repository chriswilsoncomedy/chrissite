About Chris

Chris Wilson is a comedian from Greenville, SC. Chris has Cerebral Palsy, but hes not afraid to joke about it. Its better to be laughed with and not laughed at (Chris hopes you do laugh at him hes a comedian).

Chris has shared the stage with platinum-selling rapper T.I., sold-out shows with Preacher Lawson, featured for Sam Miller on his 2025 tour, and performed with Dont Tell Comedy.

He has a comedy album, Whats Wrong With Your Ass?, available on Bandcamp, and has won Funny or Fired at The Comedy Zone Greenville three times.
